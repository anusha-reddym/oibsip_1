KOBE BRYANT

ABOUT

Kobe Bryant was a former professional basketball player who played for the Los Angeles Lakers and helped them win 5 NBA titles.Kobe Bryant was one of the greatest basketball players of all time.Born to a former NBA player, Joe Bryant, Kobe attended Lower Merion High School in Pennsylvania, where he was recognized as the top high-school basketball player in the country. Bryant started playing basketball at a young age of 3 years and the Lakers were his favorite team when he was growing up. At an early age, he also learned to play soccer. Drafted to the N.B.A. directly out of high school in 1996, Bryant was named an All-Star in 18 of his 20 seasons for the Lakers and helped lead the team to five championships.
